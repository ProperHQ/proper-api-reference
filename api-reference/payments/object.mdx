---
title: "The payout object"
---

Payments are paid by the tenants to Proper.
The payment is made to a lease, which is referenced in the payment object.
After Proper has received the payment, we will transfer the payment to the landlord, with a payout.
See /payouts for more information about payouts.

### Attributes

<ResponseField name="id" type="string">
  The unique identifier for the payment
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="paymentDate" type="datetime">
  The date the payment was made
</ResponseField>


<ResponseField name="net" type="Amount Object">
   The net amount of the payment

   <Expandable title="properties of Amount Object">

     <ResponseField name="currency" type="string">
       The currency code for the net amount (e.g., "DKK")
     </ResponseField>

     <ResponseField name="amount" type="number">
       The amount of the net amount (e.g., 100.00)
     </ResponseField>

   </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="fees" type="Amount Object">
   The fees subtracted from the payment

   <Expandable title="properties of Amount Object">

     <ResponseField name="currency" type="string">
       The currency code for the fees amount (e.g., "DKK")
     </ResponseField>

     <ResponseField name="amount" type="number">
       The amount of the fees amount (e.g., 100.00)
     </ResponseField>

   </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="gross" type="Amount Object">
   The gross amount of the payment

   <Expandable title="properties of Amount Object">

     <ResponseField name="currency" type="string">
       The currency code for the gross amount (e.g., "DKK")
     </ResponseField>

     <ResponseField name="amount" type="number">
       The amount of the gross amount (e.g., 100.00)
     </ResponseField>

   </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="payoutId" type="String">
  The payout that resulted from this payment
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="leaseId" type="String">
  The lease identifier that this payment was made to
</ResponseField>
